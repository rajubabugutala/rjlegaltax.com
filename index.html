Statutory Compliance

ESI · PF · Returns

🏥
ESI — Employee State Insurance
Registration, monthly challan generation, employee ESI card issuance, claim assistance, and half-yearly returns filing under ESIC Act 1948. Applicable for wages up to ₹21,000/month.
🛡️
PF — Provident Fund
EPF registration, UAN generation, monthly ECR filing, PF transfer and withdrawal assistance, and annual PF return under EPF & MP Act 1952. Employee: 12%, Employer: 12%.
📝
Income Tax Returns (ITR)
All ITR forms — ITR-1 to ITR-7 for individuals, HUF, firms, and companies. Timely filing, refund tracking, and response to IT department notices.
🧾
GST Returns
Monthly GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, quarterly filings, annual GSTR-9 and 9C reconciliation, ITC matching, and GST audit for eligible businesses.
✂️
TDS Returns (Form 24Q/26Q)
Quarterly TDS deduction computation, challan deposit, Form 24Q (salary) and 26Q (non-salary) filing, Form 16 & 16A generation, and TRACES compliance.
📊
Professional Tax & Labour Laws
State-wise Professional Tax registration and returns. Labour law registrations under Shops Act, Contract Labour Act, Minimum Wages Act, and Maternity Benefit Act.
